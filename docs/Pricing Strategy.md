# kalaiArangam SaaS Pricing Strategy

## Overview

kalaiArangam is a Multi-Tenant SaaS ERP platform designed for:

- Training & Coaching Institutes
- Rental Businesses
- Event Management Companies

The platform enables organizations to manage leads, operations, payments, scheduling, customers, reporting, and business workflows from a single unified system.

---

# Pricing Philosophy

The target market primarily consists of Small and Medium Businesses (SMBs) across Tamil Nadu and India.

Most businesses currently use:

- Excel Sheets
- WhatsApp
- Manual Registers
- Generic Accounting Software

The pricing model is intentionally designed to:

- Reduce adoption friction
- Enable module-based subscriptions
- Encourage upgrades as businesses grow
- Maximize retention through operational dependency

# Launch Recommendation

Maintain launch pricing:

| Plan                   | Monthly |
| ---------------------- | ------- |
| Free Trial             | 20 Days |
| Solo Module            | ₹999    |
| Business (2 Modules)   | ₹1,899  |
| Complete (All Modules) | ₹2,499  |
| Enterprise             | Custom  |

Focus differentiation on:

1. Module Access
2. User Limits
3. Branch Limits
4. Analytics Depth
5. AI Capabilities
6. Enterprise Integrations

Core operational features inside a purchased module should never be restricted. Customers must receive the complete workflow of the module they purchase to ensure adoption, retention, and long-term platform dependency. Based on kalaiArangam's modular SaaS architecture and business model.
